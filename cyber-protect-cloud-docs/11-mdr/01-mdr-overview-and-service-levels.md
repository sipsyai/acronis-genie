# Acronis Managed Detection and Response (MDR)

Acronis MDR by Acronis TRU (Acronis MDR) is a managed security service that continuously monitors endpoints, investigates suspicious activity, and supports partners in responding to confirmed security incidents. Acronis MDR operates on top of the EDR/XDR services and relies on telemetry collected by the Acronis agent.

The Acronis MDR service is provided in English only. For more details, check the Service Definition Document available in the **MDR** menu.

## Service Levels

Acronis MDR by Acronis TRU is offered in two service levels:

### Standard MDR

Standard MDR provides 24x7 monitoring and expert investigation while leaving remediation actions under partner control. Analysts confirm incidents and provide detailed remediation recommendations.

### Advanced MDR

Advanced MDR is a fully managed service where Acronis analysts actively execute mitigation and response actions, perform threat hunting, and deliver advanced reporting.

| On demand incident investigation | NO | YES |

### Standard MDR Summary

- 24/7/365 monitoring of endpoints by security experts to catch attacks
- AI-powered event triage and prioritization
- Threat containment and isolation of affected endpoints to prevent lateral movement
- Real-time in-console visibility over a prioritized list of incidents with streamlined reporting

### Advanced MDR Summary

- Includes all features of the Standard service level
- Complete remediation, including attack rollback, recovery, and closing of any security gaps
- Proactive threat hunting for emerging threats and latest known cybercrime
