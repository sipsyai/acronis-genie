Hello Sven,
Unfortunately, in the forum, we can't assist with entitlement issues as the information we have is exclusively based on the documentation.
You should raise a ticket with the team, and our entitlement team will review the situation: https://kb.acronis.com/content/8153.
According to the user guide, you should be entitled to an upgrade if you have active maintenance in ACP 15:
How can I upgrade to Acronis Cyber Protect 16?
https://care.acronis.com/s/article/73400-Acronis-Cyber-Protect-16-Licen…
Perpetual licenses (unlimited in time) are no longer available for purchase but can still be used. If you have Acronis Cyber Protect 15 perpetual licenses with active maintenance, you can upgrade management servers and agents to Acronis Cyber Protect 16. If you do not have valid maintenance for your perpetual license, you can renew it to get a free upgrade. After upgrading to Acronis Cyber Protect 16, you’ll still see your Acronis Cyber Protect 15 perpetual licenses in the Acronis Customer Portal.
https://care.acronis.com/s/article/73388-Upgrade-to-Acronis-Cyber-Protect-16?language=en_US
