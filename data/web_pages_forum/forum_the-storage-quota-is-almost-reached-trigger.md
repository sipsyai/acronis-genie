            Good day, These quota almost reach alerts are also becoming an issue for us.
We are now on Cybercloud Platform 21.07
With a few tenants with several TB of storage at a fixed amount of 80% this means we are being warned with several hundred GB of capacity still available which can be many months of use.
A few of our tenants have complained to the point where we can only turn off their reporting completely instead of just the quota related portion or at least have more fine grain control over when they receive such a message.
20% of 100GB means a warning with 20GB free.
20% of 2.5TB means a warning with a whopping 500GB free.
This has been a hot topic with all personell I have spoken to about it, we all find it a great annoyance, as do our clients.
With the amount of options we have in other aspects of Cyber Cloud it seems odd to basically have no control over something that is quite important considering either we let it spam us daily or have to disable reporting on a higher level.
Please provide this feedback to your team, hopefully they can consider implementing a solution soon.
