            Hi CS,
I'll be glad to explain how to do it the right way.
The email address that you specify during initial configuration of an account is exactly what you need to change to receive the notifications at another address.
Yes, it can be modified. You need to specify the new email address (you will get a warning that you should confirm - this by itself will not change the address; see the screenshot attached), and then you must confirm the modification with the link sent to the new email address. After that all new emails will be sent to that new address.
Let me know if you still have doubts.
Best regards,
