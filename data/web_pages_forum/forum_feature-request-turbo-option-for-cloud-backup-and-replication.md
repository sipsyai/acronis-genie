            Hello Ryan.
We don't have any feature request for that. The velocity of the backups depends on your network connection, the size of the backup, how overloaded is your system and the hardware of your devices.
What you can do is to increase the priority of the backups in the system, that can make them faster: https://www.acronis.com/en-us/support/documentation/CyberProtectionServ…
Feel free to update the thread if you have any questions.
