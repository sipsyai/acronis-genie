            Hello Fredrik!
Thank you for your questions and I apologize for the delay in the reply.
 
As I understood, you have an inquiry about the daily recap feature that was recently re-enabled in the EU1 DC after the latest update. I am attaching a screenshot for reference.

In current design, you can only turn it on and off, but you can set up a kind of reporting admin for each tenant to send daily recaps solely for the customer group to the same email address as a workaround. In Acronis Backup Cloud 8.0 the functionality will be significantly improved, we will add customization such as cross-tenant's daily reports, flexible report emails, business notifications and backup notification settings (internal reference number ABR-111214)
Acronis Backup Cloud 8.0 is under development at the moment, current ETA of the release is ~end of 2017/early 2018
Regarding your last question, in Acronis Backup Cloud 9.0 we have plans to add ability to set threshold for quota alerts (ABR-95317). We still gather feedback for this feature, so it may be moved to another release, thus, I cannot provide any ETAs at the moment.
We value feedback of our partners, so if you have any further suggestions on the improvements, please feel free to share it with us and your feedback will be provided directly to the development team.
