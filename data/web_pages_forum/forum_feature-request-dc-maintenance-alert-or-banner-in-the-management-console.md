            Hi,
I have a feature which I think would be useful. A banner alert in the partner level management console that provides information about upcoming maintenance on the partners data center. 
I think the alert show up from the time the maintenance window is announced until the maintenance is completed. I think it should not be a dismissable alert only visible to partners or customer admins.
I know that maintenance alerts are sent out by email but frequently I find partners/customers either miss or ignore them and when they cannot access their portals we get an influx of support calls.
If this could be considered as a feature I think it would be very helpful.
Thanks,
Nick
