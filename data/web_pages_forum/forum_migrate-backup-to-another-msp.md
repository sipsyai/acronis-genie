            Hello Darnel,
This type of request is a bit more complex due to the way account structure and storage are managed in Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud.
Please refer to the following Knowledge Base article for context:https://care.acronis.com/s/article/Acronis-Cyber-Protect-Cloud-how-to-move-an-account-to-another-group?language=en_US
To migrate accounts and associated backups, an internal support task is required.
Additionally, if the new MSP is using a different datacenter, the move may not be possible — this point must be confirmed by Acronis Support on a case-by-case basis.
Therefore, the next step is for the new MSP to open a Support ticket so our team can review the configuration in detail.
If you are directly entitled to Support, you may also open the request yourself.
