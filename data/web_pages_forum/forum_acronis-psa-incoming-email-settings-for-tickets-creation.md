In scope of the onboarding service we offer partners help with customer and user data migration to Acronis PSA.
Indeed, this is not available as a feature in Acronis Cyber Cloud UI, but our team can help with that. I'm attaching the import template, you can share this template with your customer and connect me with them to proceed on further steps. 
As a result, we create customers and users in Acronis Cyber Cloud in a “silent” mode, so they will be visible for MSP's team, but we don’t send them any notifications and welcome login messages. They will be visible in the service desk only. It’s possible to send them the activation emails later if needed.
