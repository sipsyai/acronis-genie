Hello Matthew. Thanks for the details.
The issue is that apart of downloading those mailboxes there's not much he can do since the agent is cloud based and that C2C is mostly made to recovery directly in the organization.
They can try also the web restore: https://kb.acronis.com/content/60087 ( there is the limitation of 100MB BUT sometimes it works even if it's not the appropriate method ).
If that doesn't work for them the last alternative is the large scale recovery, if they really need it they should give it a try: https://kb.acronis.com/content/56070.
That issue they reported is strange because why the authorities need those files if they can access them from metadata? Imagine you have a PC and the outlook/onedrive or anything else is installed in there, just with the disk it's possible to collect certain things from metadata.
