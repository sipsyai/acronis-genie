            Hello Zeljko,
Thank you for your question.
You can import protection plans created only in Cyber Protection 9.0. Plans created in the previous product versions are incompatible with the version 9.0. This applies also to protection plans from different products which the Acronis Cloud Backup 12.5 is to Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud.
I am afraid this is not possible.
