            Hello,
We have about 25 clients on Acronis backup with many of their devices and services being protected by Acronis. The product is very good, but we would like to find a better way to manage failure notifications. Some of our clients are backing up all of their workstations, for example, and that results in a large number of failure notifications that are creating tickets in our PSA.
Is there a feature where you can set it to only send a failure notification after the 3rd, 5th, etc consecutive failure? Not just for servers/workstations, but also for Sharepoint, email accounts, etc. That would be very useful.
Thanks!

            Hello!
At this moment, there isn't any feature like that, or any feature request like that to be implemented in the near future. It's not possible to send a notification after a certain number of failures. You can simply enable it for all failures or none.
Thanks in advance!
