            Hey Mark Priest
Did you purchase an Acronis Backup plan through Plesk or are you using a backup service from your hosting provider?
The login details for your account.acronis.com / forum.acronis.com will not work, you will need an account at cloud.acronis.com
Usually after signing up with Cyber Protect Cloud (either via Plesk, or via your hosting provider) you will receive an email asking you to activate your account and set a password for cloud.acronis.com
These are the credentials required for the Acronis extension in Plesk.
