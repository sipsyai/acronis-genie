What account would you like to delete?
If you wish to delete your Acronis account, please refer to this guide. (If you have any licenses there, I would advise you to move them to another account before deleting.)
If you want to delete your forum account, you can go to your profile, select "Edit," and then choose the "Cancel Account" option at the bottom. If you can't see the option, please let me know. Please be aware that once deleted, there is no way to restore the account, and you would need to start from scratch if you wish to re-enable it.
