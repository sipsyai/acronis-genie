            Hi Diego
I would strongly recommend that you log a call with the Acronis support team to assist you with this one as there are numerous scenarios you can face:
1. Migration from Acronis Storage Gateway 1.5 to Acronis Storage 2.2 - This is basically the most simple scenario but does require some complex back end configuration
2. Migration from Acronis Storage Gateway 1.5 to Acronis Storage Gateway 1.5 Also a simple scenario but will require registration wiht same UID and changing of DNS records etc
3. Migration from Acronis storage 2.2 to Acronis storage 2.2 is a complex scenario and requires advanced back end configuration
If your setup at the DC does not allow for on the fly adding of additional resources I would strongly recommend that you contact Acronis Support for assistance as they specialize in these types of scenarios and will direct you in the right direction on how to achieve this.
