# Copilot

Copilot
Copilot

Copilot is the in-product AI chat assistant that is designed to help you work faster, troubleshoot issues with less effort, and get accurate, real-time insights about your environment. Copilot uses the official Cyber Protect Cloud documentation, including licensing guide and integration guides, and Knowledge Base as a source and generates answers that assist you and guide you through tasks.

Copilot assists you by providing clear, contextual information about to a wide range of topics, including:

Understanding and using the product
How the product works
How to configure tenants and services
How to start using features with minimum effort
Answers to common how-to questions
Understanding licensing
Which licenses are needed for specific features
Step-by-step troubleshooting guidance
Instant answers powered by the Knowledge Base
On-demand references to relevant KB articles
Support for third-party integration questions, such as ConnectWise, Kaseya, and others.

Copilot is connected to backend services and can retrieve live data about workloads, alerts, and protection status. You can ask questions in natural language, such as:

Give me the list of PCs that have been offline for more than 30 days.
How many devices are protected or not protected in my environment?
I need the list of all devices with Cyber Protect.
Give me the health status of my machines.
Which workloads have been offline, paused, or failing protection tasks in the last 24 hours?
For workload ABC, what's the historical protection status over the past week?
How many workloads failed backup or protection activities today, per customer?
Which workloads haven't been updated in over 30 days?
Generate a list of all workloads that are currently not assigned any protection plan.
Give me the list of devices that have been offline for 30 days, with an applied backup plan.
Give me a list of all critical security-related alerts for the last 7 days.
What are the most critical alerts I need to address right now?
What caused this high CPU usage alert on customer workload ABC?
Which alerts are safe to ignore and which ones require immediate action?
Can you generate a daily alert summary for my top 5 customers (based on affected workloads) for the past 3 days?
Show all warning alerts for the past 24 hours.
Which customer has the most failing alerts?
Which devices are generating high CPU usage alerts?
Show alerts for customer A.
Copilot, did you detect any backup issues?

If you ask for information that does not exist or cannot be retrieved, Copilot notifies you and offers to continue without that part. This ensures accuracy and avoids misleading output.

Live support and automatic ticket creation

If Copilot cannot answer your question, it can transfer the chat to a live specialist or submit a ticket, if a specialist is currently unavailable.

Native-language chat support

You can chat with live specialists in English or in your native language. Copilot automatically translates all non-English messages, enabling smooth communication with a live specialist regardless of the language that you use.

Flexible chat experience

You can unpin the chat window and move it anywhere inside the application window. Thus, you can adjust the location of the chat to the most convenient place for you, making it easy to read responses, follow guidance, and work in the console at the same time.
