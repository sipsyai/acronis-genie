# Introduction

Introduction
Introduction

Cyber Protect Cloud is a cloud platform that enables service providers, resellers, and distributors to deliver data protection services to their partners and customers. The services are provided from the partner level down to the customer level, and at the end-user level.

Management of cloud services is available through web applications called 'service consoles'. Tenant and user account management is available through a web application called Management Portal.

This guide is intended for partner administrators who work with Management Portal. It describes how to use Management Portal to configure and manage the services that are available in Cyber Protect Cloud.

With Management Portal, you can:

Manage tenants

Manage user accounts

Access service consoles

Configure tenant services and quotas

Manage storage

Manage branding

Monitor service usage

Access reports
