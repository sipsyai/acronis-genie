# PSA

PSA

Professional Services Automation (PSA) is a business management tool for Managed Service Providers (MSPs). It is designed to make it easy and intuitive for MSPs to take advantage of business management platforms and software, and to manage and automate daily tasks, including:

Customer billing and invoicing
Customer support and service desk ticketing
Sales and project management

PSA ensures that your clients get the service they need, while you remain in full control of your operations. The components that make up PSA include ticketing, RMM integration, project management, automatic time registration, and consumption-based billing. It also provides quick and easy access to your client’s billing and ticketing data.

Key features

Manage service desk tickets

Support tickets are automatically converted from incoming emails and alerts from third-party integrated platforms, if enabled. For more information, see PSA service desk.

Manage billing

Invoices are automatically generated, based on the time spent with your client or according to the billing arrangement you have with them. For more information, see PSA sales and billing.

Manage projects

Create customer-oriented project plans with defined timelines and budgets, and assign project steps to be completed by project team members. Then monitor and track the progress of projects, and bill clients for the relevant project work. For more information, see PSA projects.

Manage time registrations and activities

Approve ticket time for billing, request time off, and approve holidays as an admin user or manager. For more information, see PSA time entries.

Native integration with services

This includes usage-based customer billing and device control with RMM.

PSA app

You can also use the dedicated mobile application for your day-to-day service desk operations. This includes monitoring and processing of tickets, and tracking and registering of work time. The PSA app can be downloaded from the App Store and Google Play Store.

PSA billing

PSA is subject to additional charges. Your account will be charged based on the number of users (or technicians) that are granted access to the PSA service.
