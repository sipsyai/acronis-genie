# Accessing Vendor Portal

Accessing Vendor Portal

Vendor Portal is an 24x7 on-line service accessible from any web browser.
It is accessed through the Acronis Management Portal integrations catalog.


Important
To access Vendor Portal through Management Portal, your organization must first register and activate a partner account, and create a Vendor Portal account for you.


To access Vendor Portal

Log in to your Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud partner account and navigate to Management Portal.
Select INTEGRATIONS from the main menu.
Click Vendor Portal, located at the top-right of the page.


Note
If you have not yet created a CyberApp, you will see this screen.
