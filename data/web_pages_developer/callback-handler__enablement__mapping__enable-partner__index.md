# Mapped partner enablement

Mapped partner enablement
To support mapped partner enablement of a CyberApp, your callback handler must be able to handle the following predefined Acronis callbacks:


Reading the partner ID from your service.
Mapping the partner ID to the Acronis tenant ID.
