# Inbound integrations (CyberApps)

Inbound integrations (CyberApps)

This chapter covers CyberApps and details how to use Vendor Portal to develop and deploy CyberApps.
It is aimed at Developer Vendor Portal accounts, some sections of this chapter are also of interest to Vendor Portal Users and Read-only users.


Note
For more information on Vendor Portal account types, see Managing a CyberApp team.


Vendor Portal

We developed Vendor Portal for our Technology Ecosystem partners to build CyberApps quickly, and in accordance with our Acronis CyberApp Standard best practices.
Vendor Portal is an online tool (no downloads, no installations, accessible from any web browser, and available 24x7), providing a low-code, GUI development and management environment.


Note
To get started with Vendor Portal, see Getting started.
