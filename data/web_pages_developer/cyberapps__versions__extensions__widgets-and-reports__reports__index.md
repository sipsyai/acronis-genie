# Reports

Reports
You can create new reports that display the CyberApp widgets.
You can also extend default Acronis reports to include the CyberApp widgets.
CyberApp reports typically include a default combination of widgets, showing critical operational information provided by the CyberApp.
CyberApp reports can be created either for the Acronis Management Portal or for the Cyber Protect Cloud console.
