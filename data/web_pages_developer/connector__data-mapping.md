# Data mapping

Data mapping
To ensure the data correspondence between Acronis Cyber Platform and your service, the connector must map the data structure of Acronis Cyber Platform types (such as tenants, alerts, etc.) to
your service types.
Additionally, to be able to match the tenant structure, the connector must have access to the tenant mapping.

Note

Tenant mapping data is received by the callback handler.
