# Opening a CyberApp

Opening a CyberApp

To open a CyberApp

Open the CyberApp list.

[In list view] Either click the row, or click the row  and select Open.
[In tile view] Click Open on the CyberApp tile.



The CyberApp opens on the OVERVIEW tab.

From here, you can access:

CYBERAPP DESCRIPTIONS
CYBERAPP VERSIONS
DEPLOYMENT
ACTIVITIES
SETTINGS
