# Widgets and reports

Widgets and reports

The widgets and reports extension point creates custom report widgets for the CyberApp, and includes these widgets in pre-existing Acronis reports or new, CyberApp-specific reports. The Acronis Management Portal or the Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud console displays the reports.
For more information, see Widgets and reports.
