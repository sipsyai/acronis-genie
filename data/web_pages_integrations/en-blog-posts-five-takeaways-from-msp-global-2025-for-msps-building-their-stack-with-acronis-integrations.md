The Acronis Ecosystem was recently on
display in Spain at MSP Global 2025 and the Acronis TRU Security Summit. Our integrations
and alliances team was thrilled to meet hundreds of our trusted technology
partners (ISVs) and thousands of managed service providers (MSPs) building
their businesses with the
Acronis Technology Ecosystem
.
As the world of managed services evolves,
one thing is clear: collaboration fuels innovation. That message resonated
strongly at this year’s MSP Global, where technology leaders came together to
share insights, explore integrations and build stronger alliances that
strengthen their ecosystem. In fact, the MSP Global event theme, “
Grow Your Ecosystem
,”
perfectly captured the essence.
For ISVs looking to join the Acronis
Ecosystem and sponsor future Acronis events, read on and find out how to get
started with the
Acronis Technology Partner Program
. The teams from
Wizer
,
OpenText
,
Cubbit
and
Computer Gross
were
visited by hundreds of MSPs seeking to learn more about their partnership with
Acronis and their booth giveaways were gone shortly after the first TRU
Security coffee break!
For MSPs, here are the five key event takeaways
for building a technology stack with Acronis integrations.
1. Integration drives efficiency
A clear theme from MSP Global: MSPs are
battling tool sprawl. Between separate RMMs, PSAs, backup, security and
compliance tools, efficiency often gets lost. On average, partners use more
than 20 tools in their stack — and Acronis integrates with most of them. That’s
where Acronis integrations deliver real value.
The Acronis Technology Ecosystem now includes over 300 integrations
, enabling
MSPs to consolidate multiple tools into a unified, efficient platform.
At the
Acronis TRU Security Summit
, MSPs heard from Acronis partner
Joshua Aaronson
, Co-Founder
of
Panda Technology
, who shared how his team reduced operational costs and
simplified workflows through Acronis integrations. If you missed the session,
learn how
Panda Technology improves troubleshooting time by over 600% by using Acronis integrations.
For our newest Integration Partners, and TRU
event Gold Sponsors,
Netbird
and
Backup Eagle
, MSP
Global was a great opportunity to showcase their integration and share their
“better together” story of efficiency with the MSP community.
Platinum sponsor
LogMeIn
had a full audience to hear
Jenny Hock
, Vice
President of Product Management, speak about the lucrative opportunity for MSPs
to efficiently offer mobile device management (MDM) services.
2. Automation brings acceleration
At MSP Global, you could sense the hunger
for automation — from onboarding and monitoring to billing and compliance. MSPs know
that every manual task eats into profit margins. The Acronis Ecosystem supports
automation through native integrations at no extra cost. For example, at the
Rewst
booth,
MSPs saw how automation is streamlining workflows and boosting profitability.
If you missed their booth, you can catch up by watching the
Rewst and Acronis integration webinar
.
Speaking of acceleration, attendees of the
Acronis TRU Security Summit enjoyed exclusive access to Ferrari Land.  Walking across to the theme park with
Thomas Van Havere
from
our latest ecosystem member
Lansweeper
,
I was very conscious of the need for acceleration as he keenly rushed off to
try out the Force One Rollercoaster!
3. A unified stack strengthens MSP service
proposition
New Acronis alliances, such as with
Seagate
,
(Platinum sponsor of TRU Security Summit) are extending value even further for
service providers. At the event, MSPs heard how the Seagate and Acronis alliance
will deliver Acronis Archival Storage, a long-term, secure, compliant and
cost-efficient S3-compatible storage solution for infrequently accessed data,
integrated with the Acronis platform. Unlike other storage vendors charging for
API and traffic, Acronis provides a controlled, predictable cost model.  When speaking with
Vincent Oostlander
and
the Seagate team, there was clear excitement about this new product launch
later this year.
4. Compliance doesn’t need to be a
beast!
Many MSPs shared that managing compliance
feels complex — especially for clients across borders and industries.  However, during MSP Global, Acronis launched
the
Compliance Navigator
tool — something worth checking out today.
It was interesting to see several
compliance software vendors exhibiting and it was great to speak with teams
from
Cynomi
and
Copla
to learn more
about their software solutions and possible integrations.
5. Ecosystem maturity and choice matters
One key insight from the Acronis TRU
Security panel session, “Ecosystems, Integrations, and the MSP Hyper-Productive
Future,” was that MSPs value mature, flexible ecosystems with broad vendor
participation. A special thank you to
Wizer
,
OpenText
,
NetBird
,
Giacom
,
and
Pax8
for contributing to Acronis panel and sharing how integrations empower MSP
productivity.
Our key message combining layered, adaptive
defences to combat ransomware resonated deeply with attendees. The “Grow Your
Ecosystem” motion is accelerating, and here at Acronis we’re actively inviting
innovative ISVs to
join the Acronis Technology Partner Program
.
Building a stronger Acronis ecosystem
— together
Participating in MSP Global 2025 reinforced
the fact that the most successful MSPs are those who build their tech stacks
around integration, automation, unified delivery and service growth. The
Acronis Ecosystem thrives on collaboration. Every integration, every
partnership, and every shared success contributes to a stronger network
supporting MSP growth worldwide.
If you attended MSP Global or TRU, you’ve
already seen what’s possible when innovation meets partnership. But the journey
doesn’t stop there — the Acronis Ecosystem continues to grow, with
new Acronis integrations

cyber protection. Explore all
