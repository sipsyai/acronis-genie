Powerful integration, with no hassle
Save development time and resources with Acronis CyberApp Standard: a unique, tech stack-independent, low-code framework for building new integrations on Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud platform, using our feature-rich WYSIWYG editor and best practices.
Ready to go? Choose your scenario from the list below, and just build it!
Automate your IT operations
You are a service provider that uses Acronis on a daily basis, and want to automate any workflow or build an integration with the internal or third-party tools you use.
Acronis API allows you to work with your tenant, get alerts, workloads, users, policies and much more.

Integrate and manage Acronis from your platform
You are a technology platform vendor or distributor that wants to add Acronis services into your portfolio.
The powerful Acronis API allows you to provision a new Acronis tenant, add users, synchronize Acronis alerts to your platform, get statuses of Acronis agents on devices, manage protection plans, get Acronis product usage statistics and automate billing.

Integrate your services in Acronis
You are a technology vendor that wants to offer your service to Acronis partners.
With CyberApp standard, you can quickly build an integration that adds your service into the Acronis application catalog, and you can extend the Acronis platform with your own alerts, widgets, reports, and agent statuses. You can create, test and deploy your CyberApp in a dedicated sandbox, and fully manage its lifecycle. All this is available via a dedicated Vendor Portal.

To start building your apps and get access to your sandbox, you need to be registered in the
Acronis Technology Partner Program
. You can do it by clicking on the
“Start building now”
button on the top of this page. After registering, you will get a partner tenant in
Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud
, and a vendor portal deployed in that tenant where you can start building your first application on the Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud platform.
