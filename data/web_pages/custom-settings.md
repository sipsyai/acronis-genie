Custom settings

In specific cases, the Support team may help you resolve issues by instructing you to change settings in the protection plan.

Use Custom settings only as instructed by the Support team. Adding an incorrect custom setting may negatively affect your workload, and the service provider will not be held liable for that.

To configure Custom settings

In the Create protection plan window, expand the Antivirus & Antimalware protection module.

Click Custom settings.

In the Custom settings section, click Add.

In the value field, enter the custom setting provided by the Support team.

Configure the True/False switch as instructed by the Support team.

Click the check mark on the right to save the custom setting.

In the Add custom setting dialog, click Add.

Click Done.

The custom setting is now applied to the protection plan.
