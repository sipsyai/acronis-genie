# Acronis Managed Detection and Response (MDR)

Acronis Managed Detection and Response (MDR)
Acronis Managed Detection and Response (MDR)

Acronis MDR by TRU (Acronis MDR) is a managed security service that continuously monitors endpoints, investigates suspicious activity, and supports partners in responding to confirmed security incidents. Acronis MDR operates on top of the EDR/XDR services and relies on telemetry collected by the Acronis agent.

The Acronis MDR service is provided in English only.

For more details, check the Service Definition Document available in the MDR menu.

Service Levels

Acronis MDR by Acronis TRU (Acronis MDR) is offered in the following service levels.

Standard MDR

Standard MDR provides 24×7 monitoring and expert investigation while leaving remediation actions under partner control. Analysts confirm incidents and provide detailed remediation recommendations.

Advanced MDR

Advanced MDR is a fully managed service where Acronis analysts actively execute mitigation and response actions, perform threat hunting, and deliver advanced reporting.
