# Revoking an archiving plan

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Archiving plans > Revoking an archiving plan
Revoking an archiving plan

You can revoke an archiving plan without deleting it.

To revoke an archiving plan

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Devices > Mail servers.
On the main screen, click the name of the mail server.
On the Protect tab, click the ellipsis icon (...) next to the name of the archiving plan, and then click Revoke.

As a result, the plan will no longer be applied to the mail server and the email archive will no longer be updated.
