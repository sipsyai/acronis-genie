# Deleting buckets

Additional Cyber Protection tools > Archival storage > Managing buckets in archival storage > Deleting buckets
Deleting buckets

You can delete buckets in the Cyber Protect console.

Prerequisites

The bucket is empty.

You cannot delete an object from the bucket until the retention period for that object expires.

To delete buckets

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Backup storage > Archival storage.
Select the checkbox for the buckets that you want to delete.
Click Delete.
Select the checkbox to confirm your choice.
Enter your login to confirm your account.
Click Delete.
