# Deleting a legal hold rule

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Legal hold rules > Deleting a legal hold rule
Deleting a legal hold rule

You can delete a legal hold rule.

To delete a legal hold rule

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Legal hold rules.
Select a legal hold rule, and then click Delete.
To confirm your choice, select the checkbox, and then click Delete.

As a result, the legal hold rule is deleted and will no longer be applied to the email archive.
