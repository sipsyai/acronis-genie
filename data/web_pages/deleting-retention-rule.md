# Deleting a retention rule

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Retention rules > Deleting a retention rule
Deleting a retention rule

You can delete a retention rule.

To delete a retention rule

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Retention rules.
Select a retention rule, and then click Delete.
To confirm your choice, select the checkbox, and then click Delete.

As a result, the retention rule is deleted and will no longer be applied to the email archive.
