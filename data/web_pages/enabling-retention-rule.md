# Enabling a retention rule

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Retention rules > Enabling a retention rule
Enabling a retention rule

A retention rule is automatically enabled when you create it.

You can manually enable a retention rule that you previously disabled.

To enable a retention rule

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Retention rules.
Select the checkbox next to the name of a disabled retention rule, and then click Enable.
To confirm your choice, click Enable.

As a result, the retention rule is enabled and will be applied to the email archive the next time it runs.

Retention rules run on their email archives once every 24 hours. This operation is not user-configurable.
