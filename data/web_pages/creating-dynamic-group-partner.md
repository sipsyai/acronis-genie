Creating a dynamic device group at the partner level

You can create dynamic device groups at the partner level (All devices).

To create a dynamic device group at the partner level

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Devices > Machines with agents.
In the tree, click Machines with agents to expand the list.
Click All.

In the search field, specify the criteria according to which you want to create a dynamic device group, and then click Search.

To learn more about the available search criteria, see Search attributes for non-cloud-to-cloud workloads and Search attributes for cloud-to-cloud workloads.

Click Save as, and then specify the group name.

Add a description.

Click OK.
