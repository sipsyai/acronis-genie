# Deleting credentials

RMM > Connecting to workloads for remote desktop or remote assistance > Workload credentials > Deleting credentials
Deleting credentials

You can delete credentials that are not needed anymore.

To delete credentials from the Credentials store

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Devices > All devices.

Access the Settings menu in one of the following ways:

Click Remote desktop, and then click Settings.

Click Manage, and then click Settings.

On the tab of the supported protocol (NEAR, RDP, or Apple Screen Sharing), click Delete.
In the confirmation window, click Delete.
