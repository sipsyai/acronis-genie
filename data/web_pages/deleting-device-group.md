# Deleting a group

Managing workloads in the Cyber Protect console > Device groups > Deleting a group
Deleting a group

When you delete a device group, all plans that are applied to that group will be revoked. The workloads in the group will become unprotected if no other plans are applied to them.

To delete a device group

Click Devices, and then navigate to the group that you want to delete.
Click the gear icon next to the name of the group, and then click Delete.
Confirm your choice by clicking Delete.
