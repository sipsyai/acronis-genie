Removing baselines from a template

You can remove baselines from custom baseline templates.

To remove a baseline from a template

Required roles: Partner administrator.

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Microsoft 365 management > Baseline templates.

In the list of baseline templates, click the one that contains the baseline to remove.

The template details appear to the right, and the list of baselines is displayed on the Baselines tab.

Click the context menu at the end of the baseline's row and select Remove.

The Remove baseline dialog opens.

Click Remove to confirm.
