RMM > Monitoring the health and performance of workloads > Monitoring alerts > Viewing the alert log of monitoring alerts
Viewing the alert log of monitoring alerts

You can see all events that are related to a monitoring alert in a chronological order: the response actions (both automatic or manual) that were performed, and the email notifications that were sent.

To view the alert log of a monitoring alert

In the Protection console, go to Alerts.
Open the Table view.
In the list of alerts, click the monitoring alert that you want to view.
Click Details, and then click Alert log.
