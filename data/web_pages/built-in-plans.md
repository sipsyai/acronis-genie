Built-in plans

Build-in plans are plans that are preconfigured with some of the most commonly used or recommended settings. Built-in plans are available for selection out of the box. You cannot change built-in plans, but after applying a built-in plan to a workload, you can edit the settings.

Built-in plans are available for the following plan types: protection plans, monitoring plans, and remote management plans.
