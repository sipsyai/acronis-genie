Configuring encryption in the protection plan

In a protection plan, encryption is enabled by default. The AES-256 algorithm is used.

With a strong password, the AES-256 algorithm provides quantum-resistant encryption.

For accounts in Compliance mode, you cannot configure encryption in the protection plan. For more information on how to configure encryption on the protected device, see Configuring encryption as a machine property.

To configure encryption

In a protection plan, expand the Backup module.
In Encryption, click Specify password.
Specify and confirm the encryption password.
Click OK.
There is no way to recover encrypted backups if you lose or forget the password.

You cannot change the encryption settings after you apply the protection plan. To use different encryption settings, create a new plan.
