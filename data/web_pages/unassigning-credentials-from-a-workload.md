RMM > Connecting to workloads for remote desktop or remote assistance > Workload credentials > Unassigning credentials from a workload
Unassigning credentials from a workload

You can unassign credentials from a workload, but still keep them in the Credentials store.

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Devices > All devices.

Access the Settings menu in one of the following ways:

Click Remote desktop, and then click Settings.

Click Manage, and then click Settings.

On the tab of the supported protocol (NEAR, RDP, or Apple Screen Sharing), click Unassign.
In the confirmation window, click Unassign.
