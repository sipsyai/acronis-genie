# Previewing emails

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Recovering data from an email archive > Previewing emails
Previewing emails

You can preview archived emails without recovering them first.

To preview emails

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Backup storage > Cloud application backups, and then select the email archive that you want to search.
Click Browse archive.
[If a privacy warning is shown] In the Privacy warning dialog, click Proceed.
In the Encryption password dialog, specify the password, and then click Proceed.

Search for the emails that you want to preview.

You can use ad-hoc search queries and saved search queries. For more information, see Searching for emails.

Select the email that you want to preview.

As a result, the email is shown and you can check its content and metadata.
