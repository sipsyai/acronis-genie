# Deleting tenant baselines

RMM > Managing and monitoring the Microsoft 365 environment > Working with baselines > Deleting tenant baselines
Deleting tenant baselines

You can delete baselines that are not managed by baseline templates.

To delete baselines

Required role: Partner administrator.

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Microsoft 365 management > Baselines.

Select the check box in the row of the baselines that you want to delete.

Click Delete above the list.

In the Delete baselines dialog, click Confirm, and then click Close.

Baselines that are not managed by templates are deleted. Baselines that are part of baseline templates will not be deleted.
