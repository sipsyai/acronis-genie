# Revoking a plan from a group

Managing workloads in the Cyber Protect console > Device groups > Revoking a plan from a group
Revoking a plan from a group

You can revoke a plan from a group by selecting the group first, and then revoking the plan from it.

Alternatively, you can open the plan for editing, and then remove the group from it.

To revoke a plan from a group

Click Devices, and then navigate to the group from which you want to revoke a plan.

[For non-cloud-to-cloud workloads] Click Protect group.

A list of plans that are applied to the group is shown.

[For cloud-to-cloud workloads] Click Group backup.

A list of backup plans that are applied to the group is shown.

Select the plan that you want to revoke.
[For non-cloud-to-cloud workloads] Click the ellipsis icon (...), and then click Revoke.
[For cloud-to-cloud workloads] Click the gear icon, and then click Revoke.
