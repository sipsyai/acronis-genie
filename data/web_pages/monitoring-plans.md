# Monitoring plans

RMM > Monitoring the health and performance of workloads > Monitoring plans
Monitoring plans

Monitoring plans are plans that you apply on your managed workloads to enable and configure the monitoring functionality.

If no monitoring plan is applied on a workload, the monitoring features will not be available for the workload.

The availability of this feature and the settings that you can configure in the monitoring plan depends on the license that you have.
