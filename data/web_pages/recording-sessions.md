Recording and playing remote sessions

You can record a remote session via NEAR in Acronis Connect Client.

To record a remote session

On the Viewer toolbar in Connect Client, click Other, and select Start Recording.

Select a name and location for the record.

By default, the file will be named with the current date and time and located in the Documents folder in the current user home directory. While the recording is active, in the Viewer toolbar you will see a flashing red circle over the top right corner of the remote screen and the recording timer.

To stop the recording, click Other, and then click Stop Recording. On a Mac, you can also click Stop on the toolbar.

All .crec files made by Acronis Connect Client will be opened with Acronis Connect Client by default.

To play a recording

Locate a recording file.

Open it.

The recording player of Acronis Connect Client opens. Note that it is not possible to navigate through the recording. To find a certain moment in the recording, wait until the player reaches it.

To adjust playback speed, use the << and >> icons in the playback controls section.

The recording is stored as a sequence of events that have been transmitted to and from the remote server during a connection. This ensures the best quality of the recording at the minimum file size. However, this also means that it is not possible to navigate through the recording. At the moment it is also not possible to convert the recordings to a video format.
