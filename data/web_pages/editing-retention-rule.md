# Editing a retention rule

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Retention rules > Editing a retention rule
Editing a retention rule

You can edit a retention rule.

To edit a retention rule

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Retention rules.
Select a retention rule, and then click Edit.
Edit the retention rule, and then click Save.

As a result, the retention rule is edited. The updated settings will be applied to the email archive the next time the retention rule runs.

Retention rules run on their email archives once every 24 hours. This operation is not user-configurable.
