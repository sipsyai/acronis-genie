# Revoking monitoring plans

RMM > Monitoring the health and performance of workloads > Monitoring plans > Revoking monitoring plans
Revoking monitoring plans

You can revoke a monitoring plan from a workload to which the plan was applied.

Prerequisites

At least one monitoring plan is applied to the workload.

To revoke a monitoring plan

In the Protection console, go to Devices > All devices.
Click the workload, and then click Protect.
Click the More actions icon of the monitoring plan that you want to revoke, and then click Revoke.
