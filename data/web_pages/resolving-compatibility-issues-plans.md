Working with plans > Compatibility issues with plans > Resolving compatibility issues
Resolving compatibility issues

Depending on the cause of the compatibility issues, you can perform different actions to resolve the compatibility issues as part of the process of creating or editing a plan.

To resolve the compatibility issues

Conflicting plan
Incompatible OS
Unsupported agent
Insufficient quota
Incompatible workload type
Missing advanced pack

Click Review issues.

[To resolve the issue by removing workloads from the new plan]

On the Conflicting plan tab, select the workloads that you want to remove.

You cannot remove workloads that are part of a device group.

Click Remove selected workloads from the plan.

Click Remove, and then click Close.

[To resolve the issue by disabling the plans that are already applied to the workload]

Click Disable applied plans.
Click Disable, and then click Close.

[To resolve the issue by disabling the conflicting modules in the plans that are already applied to the workload]

Click Disable conflicting modules in applied plans.
Click Disable, and then click Close.
