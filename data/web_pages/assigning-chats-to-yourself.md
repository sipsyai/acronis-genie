Assigning chat sessions to yourself

You can assign to yourself chat session that are Unassigned or Assigned to others.

To assign a chat session to yourself

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Chat.
Locate the chat session that you want to reassign, and then click it.

Click Assign to me.

The chat session is assigned to you. You can now send messages in this chat.

If you are offline or away from the console for 15 minutes, and the chat is not put on hold, the system automatically changes the status of the chat to Unassigned.
