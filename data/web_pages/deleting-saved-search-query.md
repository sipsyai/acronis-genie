# Deleting a saved search query

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Search queries > Deleting a saved search query
Deleting a saved search query

You can delete a saved search query from the email archive or from the Management > Archiving plans tab.

To delete a search query

From the email archive
From Management > Archiving plans
Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Backup storage > Backups > Cloud application backups.
[If you want to search an archive in immutable storage] Click Show deleted.
Select an email archive, and then click Browse archive.
[If a privacy warning is shown] In the Privacy warning dialog, click Proceed.
In the Encryption password dialog, specify the password, and then click Proceed.
Under Saved search queries, click the gear icon next to the search query, and then click Delete.

To confirm your choice, click Delete.

Deleting a search query affects the retention rules and legal hold rules in which this search query is used.
