# Deleting SIEM plan

Working with plans > SIEM forwarding plans > Deleting SIEM plan
Deleting SIEM plan

When you delete a SIEM forwarding plan, data forwarding ceases, but the data which has already been forwarded is not deleted.

To delete a SIEM forwarding plan

Open your partner SIEM forwarding plan list or the customer-specific list.

Click on the plan you want to delete.

The details and actions sidebar opens.

Click Delete.

Select the confirmation checkbox to enable the Confirm button.


Click Confirm.
