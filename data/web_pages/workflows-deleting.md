# Deleting a workflow

Managing and assigning automation workflows > Managing your workflows > Deleting a workflow
Deleting a workflow

As the Company administrator, Administrator, or Cyber administrator, you can delete workflows that you created or that were assigned to you.

You can delete only workflows in Draft state.

To delete a workflow

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Management > Workflows.

Click the My workflows switch to display the workflows you created, and any workflows assigned to you.

In the rightmost column of the relevant workflow row, click the ellipsis (...) icon, and then select Delete.

In the confirmation message, click Delete.
