GenAI protection widgets

GenAI protection widgets provide visibility into how endpoints access generative AI services. Use them to identify the most popular AI solutions, the devices with the highest activity, and recent usage details.

Widgets related to GenAI Protection are available in both Management Portal and the Cyber Protect console. The widgets that you can add and view depend on your role and on the currently selected level - partner or customer.

Top AI solutions (visits)

This widget shows the statistics of Generative AI usage by solution.

Generative AI usage summary (devices)

This widget is available at customer level. It shows the summary of Generative AI usage for a specified time.

Generative AI usage summary (customers)

This widget is available at partner level. It shows the summary of Generative AI usage for a specified time.

Generative AI usage details (past 7 days)

This widget shows the detailed information of Generative AI usage for the past 7 days.

Generative AI usage (visits per device)

This widget is available at customer level. It shows the statistics of Generative AI usage by device.

Generative AI usage (visits per customer)

This widget is available at partner level. It shows the statistics of Generative AI usage by customer.

Sensitive data transfer trend

This widget shows the dynamics of allowed and blocked sensitive data transfers.

Top devices with DLP incidents

This widget shows devices with the highest number of sensitive data transfers.
