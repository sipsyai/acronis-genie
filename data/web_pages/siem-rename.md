# Renaming a SIEM forwarding plan

Working with plans > SIEM forwarding plans > Renaming a SIEM forwarding plan
Renaming a SIEM forwarding plan

To rename a SIEM forwarding plan

Open your partner SIEM forwarding plan list or the customer-specific list.

Click on the plan you want to rename.

The details and actions sidebar opens.

Click Rename.

Edit the plan name.


Click Rename.
