Applying a protection plan to a workload

To protect a workload, you must apply a protection plan to it.

You can apply a plan from the Devices tab and from the Management > Protection plans tab.

Devices
Management > Protection plans
Select one or more workloads that you want to protect.
Click Protect.

[If another protection plan was already applied to the selected workloads] Click Add plan.

A list of available protection plans is shown.

Select the protection plan that you want to apply, and then click Apply.

To learn how to apply a protection plan to a device group, see Applying a plan to a group.
