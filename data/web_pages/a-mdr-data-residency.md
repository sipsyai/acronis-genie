Data Residency and Global Service Delivery for Acronis MDR

Acronis MDR is designed to deliver global, 24/7 threat detection and response while balancing data residency, scalability, and operational excellence. This article explains how data is stored and processed as part of the service, and how Acronis delivers MDR consistently across regions.

Overview

Acronis MDR uses a distributed architecture that separates endpoint telemetry, security investigations, and service operations. This approach enables:

Regional data storage where applicable.
Efficient global threat hunting and correlation.
Continuous 24/7 service delivered by a follow-the-sun SOC model.
Where Your Data Is Stored
Endpoint and Product Data
Product and telemetry data generated by Acronis security products is stored in Acronis regional data centers, aligned with the customer’s selected region.

Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) operational data is stored in three global processing regions:

United States, Europe, and AsiaPacific (Singapore).

This model ensures performance, resilience, and regional availability while supporting advanced detection capabilities.
Security Events Used for Threat Hunting

Security events used for proactive threat hunting are processed in a centralized analytics environment located in Europe.

This centralized model enables crossregion correlation, faster detection of emerging threats, and consistent investigation quality across all customers.

Event data can be logically segmented to support customers operating in different geographies.
MDR Investigations and Case Management

MDR investigation records, including analyst findings and customer communications, are stored in the Acronis' centralized case management system hosted in Europe.

This includes:

Incident investigation results
Analyst notes and verdicts
Customer communications related to MDR cases
Centralized case management ensures consistent service quality, auditability, and end-to-end traceability of security incidents.
Global SOC Operations

Acronis MDR is delivered by a globally distributed team of security analysts (Level 1 and Level 2) operating under a unified service model.

Analysts are located across multiple regions worldwide.
The service follows a follow-the-sun model, ensuring continuous monitoring and response regardless of customer location.
This approach allows Acronis to provide 24/7 coverage without relying on country-specific SOC teams, while maintaining consistent processes and service levels.
Benefits for your customers

Consistent MDR experience worldwide:

Customers receive the same investigation quality, response processes, and reporting regardless of region.

Scalable and resilient service:

Centralized analytics and case management allow Acronis to rapidly scale MDR coverage and adapt to evolving threats.

Regional alignment where it matters:

Product and endpoint data are stored in regional Acronis data centers, while security analytics leverage global intelligence to improve detection and response.

Designed for global organizations:

The architecture supports customers with distributed environments, multi-region operations, and MSPs serving clients across multiple countries.
