Downloading a licensing report for email archiving

You can download a licensing report for email archiving at the partner level and at the customer level.

In customer tenants, this report is only available if a Microsoft 365 mail server is added.

To download a licensing report

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as an administrator.
Click the account icon in the upper-right corner.

Click Email archiving licensing report.

A ZIP file is downloaded to your machine. The ZIP file contains the licensing report, in the CSV format.
