Removing plans from favorites

You can remove favorite protection plans, monitoring plans, and remote management plans from the list of favorites.

The feature is not available for users who are assigned the Read-only administrator role for the Protection service.

Protection plans
Remote management plans
Monitoring plans

Prerequisites

At least one protection plan is set as favorite for your tenant.

To remove a protection plan from the favorites

On the Protection plans screen, click the plan that you want to remove from favorites, and then click Details.

Click the ellipsis icon (...), and then click Remove from favorites.

On the Protection plans screen, the star icon next to the plan name disappears.
