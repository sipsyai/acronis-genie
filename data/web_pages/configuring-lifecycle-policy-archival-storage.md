# Configuring a lifecycle policy

Additional Cyber Protection tools > Archival storage > Managing buckets in archival storage > Configuring a lifecycle policy
Configuring a lifecycle policy

To delete unnecessary files automatically, you can configure a lifecycle policy at the bucket level. For more information, see Lifecycle policy.

To configure a lifecycle policy

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Backup storage > Archival storage.
Click the bucket that you want to edit.
On the Lifecycle tab, turn on the Lifecycle switch.

[For versioned buckets] Select the versions to which you want to apply the lifecycle policy.

Configure the period after which the lifecycle policy will be applied to the objects in the bucket.

A lifecycle policy will not be applied to objects in retention. Configure a lifecycle period that is longer than the retention period for the objects that you want to delete.
Click Save.
