Additional actions with software deployment plans

The availability of this feature depends on the license that you have.

From the Software deployment plans screen, you can perform the following additional actions with software deployment plans: view details, edit, view the activities, view the alerts, rename, clone, export, import, and delete.

Prerequisites

2FA is enabled for your user account.
You have one of the following roles for Cyber Protection: Company administrator or Cyber administrator.
