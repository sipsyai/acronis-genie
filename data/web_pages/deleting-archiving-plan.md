# Deleting an archiving plan

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Archiving plans > Deleting an archiving plan
Deleting an archiving plan

You can delete an archiving plan.

To delete an archiving plan

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans, and then click Archiving plans.
On the main screen, click the plan name, and then click Delete.
To confirm your decision, select the checkbox, and then click Delete.

As a result, the archiving plan will be deleted. The email archive will not be deleted, but it will no longer be updated.
