# Editing a SIEM forwarding plan

Working with plans > SIEM forwarding plans > Editing a SIEM forwarding plan
Editing a SIEM forwarding plan

You cannot change the Method. To change it, you must delete the SIEM forwarding plan and create a new one.


To edit a SIEM forwarding plan

Open your partner SIEM forwarding plan list or the customer-specific list.

Click on the plan you want to edit.

The details and actions sidebar opens.

Click Edit.

Make your changes and click Confirm.
