# Disabling a legal hold rule

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Legal hold rules > Disabling a legal hold rule
Disabling a legal hold rule

A disabled legal hold rule is not applied to the email archive. The disabled legal hold rule is not deleted and you can enable it later.

To disable a legal hold rule

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Legal hold rules.
Select the checkbox next to the name of a legal hold rule, and then click Disable.
To confirm your choice, click Disable.

As a result, the legal hold rule will no longer be applied to the email archive.
