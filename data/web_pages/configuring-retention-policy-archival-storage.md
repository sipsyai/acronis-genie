# Configuring a retention policy

Additional Cyber Protection tools > Archival storage > Managing buckets in archival storage > Configuring a retention policy
Configuring a retention policy

In buckets where object locking is enabled, you can configure a retention policy. For more information, see Retention policy.

To configure a retention policy

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Backup storage > Archival storage.
Click the bucket that you want to edit.
On the Object locking tab, turn on the Retention switch.

In Retention mode, select the mode.

In compliance mode, objects cannot be deleted during their retention period by any user, including administrators.

In Retention period, specify the period in days or years.

You can specify a period of between 1 day and 100 years.

Click Save.
