Using the MDR service portal

Once an incident is confirmed, the escalation contacts receive an email notification with a link to the MDR Service Portal.

The MDR Service Portal is the primary interface for interacting with MDR incidents.

To view resolved and active incidents

Log in to the Acronis MDR service portal.

The MDR team has provided the link to the service portal by email.

On the welcome screen, click View MDR incidents.

.

You can filter incidents by status, request type, customer, and more.

To control the columns that appear in the list, click Edit list view.

To review analyst findings and actions

In the list of incidents, click the incident that you want to review.

Check the incident details.

To add comments, request additional investigation, or request incident reopening

In the Comments box of the incident details view, add a comment to explain your request.
