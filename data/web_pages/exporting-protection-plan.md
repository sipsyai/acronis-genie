# Exporting a protection plan

Working with plans > Protection plans and modules > Actions with protection plans > Exporting a protection plan
Exporting a protection plan

You can export a protection plan in a JSON file.

This feature is not available for plans at the partner level. For more information about this level, see Administration levels in the Cyber Protect console.

Prerequisites

At least one protection plan is available for your tenant.

To export a protection plan

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Management > Protection plans.

Click the plan that you want to export, and in the Actions menu, click Export.

A JSON file that contains the configuration is created and saved to the default download folder on your machine.
