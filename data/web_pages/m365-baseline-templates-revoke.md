# Revoking baseline templates

RMM > Managing and monitoring the Microsoft 365 environment > Managing tenant baseline templates > Revoking baseline templates
Revoking baseline templates

You can revoke a template from one or more customers on which it is deployed.

To revoke a template from a customer

Required roles: Partner administrator.

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Microsoft 365 management > Baseline templates.

In the list of baseline templates, click the one that you want to revoke.

The template details appear to the right.

Click the Customers tab.

In the list of customers, click the trash bin to revoke the template from the corresponding customer.

Click Confirm.
