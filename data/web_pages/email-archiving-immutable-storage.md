Immutable storage for email archiving

With immutable storage, you can access the following deleted items during the retention period of the immutable storage:

Email messages that were automatically deleted by retention rules.
Email archives that were deleted by an administrator, and the email messages in these archives.

You can browse and search the deleted email messages, recover them, and undo their deletion.

You cannot apply retention rules and legal hold rules to deleted items.

After the retention period ends, the items in immutable storage are deleted permanently. For more information, see Immutable storage.
