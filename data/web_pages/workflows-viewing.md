Viewing current workflows

To view all workflows, in the Cyber Protect console, go to Management > Workflows.

Partner or tenant users with the role of Company administrator, Administrator, or Cyber administrator, can view and update any workflows in the system.

If there are no existing workflows, you are prompted to create one. For more information, see Creating a workflow.

The following Information is displayed for each workflow:

The name and current enabled version of the workflow.
A user-defined description of the workflow.
The current status of the workflow (either Enabled, Disabled, or Draft).
The date and time when the workflow was last executed.
The status of the last execution (either Success, Error, or Not executed).
The user who created the workflow.

Click on a workflow to view additional details in the right pane. This information includes a general overview, access to the execution history, the tenants who can work with it (only available for the Company administrator, Administrator, or Cyber administrator), and the current available versions.

You can also click the My workflows switch to filter the displayed list to show only those workflows relevant for your role. For example, if you are a Company administrator, Administrator, or Cyber administrator, you will see only the workflows you created. If you are a user or read-only administrator, you will see those workflows assigned to you.
