# Incident detection and handling

Acronis Managed Detection and Response (MDR) > Incident detection and handling
Incident detection and handling

When suspicious activity is detected by EDR or XDR, MDR analysts investigate and determine whether it represents a true security incident.

Notifications and escalation

Once an incident is confirmed, the escalation contacts receive an email notification with a link to the MDR Service Portal.

Critical incidents may trigger a phone escalation.
Response and Mitigation Actions

Depending on the service level, severity, approvals, and licensing, MDR may isolate endpoints, stop malicious processes, quarantine files, or perform recovery actions.

Threat Hunting
Threat hunting is available with Advanced MDR only.

Advanced MDR includes monthly proactive threat hunting activities designed to identify advanced or stealthy threats that may not trigger standard alerts.

Reporting
Standard MDR Reports

Standard MDR customers receive monthly operational reports covering deployment status, incidents, and detection statistics.

Advanced MDR Reports

Advanced MDR customers receive executive summaries, detailed incident reports, root cause analysis, and threat hunting reports.
