Additional actions with chat sessions

Depending on the status of a chat session, you can perform the following additional actions: put on hold, resume, end, reopen, remote workload actions.

Put on hold
Resume
End chat
Reopen
Remote actions

You can temporarily pause an active chat session when you need more time to reply. For example, to find the solution to the user's issue.

To put a chat session on hold

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Chat.
Click the active chat that you want to put on hold.

Click the ellipsis button (...), and then click Put on hold.

The chat session is put on hold. The On hold tag is displayed for this chat in the chat list.
