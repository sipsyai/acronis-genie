# Editing an archiving plan

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Archiving plans > Editing an archiving plan
Editing an archiving plan

You can edit the name of an archiving plan.

To edit an archiving plan

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Email archiving plans.
Click the plan name, and then click Edit.
To change the plan name, click the pencil icon, specify a new name, and then click OK.
Click Save.
