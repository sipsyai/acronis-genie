# Recovering a machine with One-click recovery

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Backup options > One-click recovery > Recovering a machine with One-click recovery
Recovering a machine with One-click recovery
Prerequisites

A protection plan with enabled One-click recovery backup option is applied to the machine.

There is at least one disk backup of the machine.

To recover a machine

Reboot the machine that you want to recover.

During the reboot, press F11 to enter Startup Recovery Manager.

The rescue media window opens.

Select Acronis Cyber Protect.

[If a recovery password was specified in the protection plan] Enter the recovery password, and then click OK.

Select a One-click recovery option.
To recover the latest backup automatically, select the first option, and then click OK.

To recover another backup within the backup archive, select the second option, and then click OK.

Confirm your choice by clicking Yes.

The rescue media window opens, and then disappears. The recovery procedure continues without it.

[If you chose to recover a specific backup] Select the backup that you want to recover, and then click OK.

After a while, the recovery starts and its progress is shown. When the recovery completes, your machine reboots.
