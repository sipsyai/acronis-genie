# Disabling a retention rule

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Retention rules > Disabling a retention rule
Disabling a retention rule

A disabled retention rule is not applied to the email archive. The disabled retention rule is not deleted, and you can enable it later.

To disable a retention rule

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Management > Archiving plans > Retention rules.
Select the checkbox next to the name of a retention rule, and then click Disable.
To confirm your choice, click Disable.

As a result, the retention rule will no longer be applied to the email archive.
