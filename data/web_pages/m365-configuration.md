Configuring Microsoft 365 connections
This feature is only available for Partner administrator users.

When Microsoft 365 security monitoring and management is enabled in Cyber Protect, you can connect to the relevant Microsoft 365 clients associated with your (or your customer's) Microsoft account.

You can then map the Microsoft 365 tenants to the relevant customers in Cyber Protect.
