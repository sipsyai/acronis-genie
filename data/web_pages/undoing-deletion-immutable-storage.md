# Undoing deletion of emails in immutable storage

Managing the backup and recovery of workloads and files > Email archiving > Immutable storage for email archiving > Undoing deletion of emails in immutable storage
Undoing deletion of emails in immutable storage

You can undo the deletion of emails that are in immutable storage, and restore them to the email archive.

You can undo deletion of individual emails. Undoing deletion of email archives is not supported.

To undo the deletion

Log in to the Cyber Protect console as administrator.
Go to Backup storage > Cloud application backups.
[If the whole email archive is deleted] Click Show deleted.
Select an email archive, and then click Browse archive.
[If a privacy warning is shown] In the Privacy warning dialog, click Proceed.
In the Encryption password dialog, specify the password, and then click Proceed.

Search for specific emails using the search field. For more information, see Searching for emails.

Alternatively, you can use a search query that includes deleted items. For more information, see Creating a saved search query.

Select up to 10 deleted emails, and then click Undo deletion.

As a result, the selected emails will no longer be marked as deleted and will be restored to the email archive.
