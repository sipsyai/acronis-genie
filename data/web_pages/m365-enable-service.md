# Disabling the Microsoft 365 management service

RMM > Managing and monitoring the Microsoft 365 environment > Configuring Microsoft 365 connections > Disabling the Microsoft 365 management service
Disabling the Microsoft 365 management service

You can disable the Microsoft 365 management service for customers previously onboarded (see Connecting to a Microsoft account) in Cyber Protect Cloud.

To disable the Microsoft 365 management service

In the Cyber Protect console, go to Microsoft 365 management > Configuration.
Click the Settings tab, and then click Disable service.

In the confirmation dialog, click Disable.

Disabling the service will remove:

The connected Acronis application from the App registrations directory in your tenant's Microsoft Entra ID.

All customer-related data stored on Cyber Protect Cloud, including the mapping of customers to Microsoft 365 tenants.
