Setting plans as favorite

You can set up to 10 favorite plans per supported plan type (protection plan, monitoring plan, or remote management plan) per tenant.

The feature is not available for users who are assigned the Read-only administrator role for the Protection service.

Remote management plans

Prerequisites

At least one protection plan is created for your tenant. For more information, see Creating a protection plan.

To set a protection plan as favorite

On the Protection plans screen, click the plan that you want to set as favorite, and then click Details.

Click the ellipsis icon (...), and then click Add to favorites.

On the Protection plans screen, a star icon appears next to the plan name.
